# Product Overview

## What It Is
A web and mobile application that empowers homeowners and renters to optimize and transform their living spaces through intelligent, AI-powered guidance — without the high cost of hiring a professional interior designer.

## The Problem
Professional interior designers charge $2,000–$10,000+ per project, putting expert space design out of reach for most households. The result: underutilized rooms, awkward furniture placement, poor lighting choices, and homes that simply don't function as well as they could — all because people lack accessible, personalized guidance.

## The Solution
An accessible home space optimization platform that delivers interior design intelligence on demand:
- Room measurement, scanning, and spatial analysis tools
- AI-generated furniture layout and arrangement suggestions
- Color palette, material, and décor recommendations
- Personalized style profiling to match recommendations to the user's taste
- Step-by-step room transformation guides users can execute themselves
- Budget prioritization — highest-impact changes within the user's real budget

## Target Users
Homeowners and renters who want to genuinely improve how their living spaces look and function, but cannot or choose not to spend thousands on a professional interior designer. Budget-conscious, design-curious, and practical.

## Positioning
Democratizing interior design. Professional-quality space guidance at a fraction of the cost, available to anyone with a phone.

## Tone & Brand Voice
Approachable, empowering, and practical. Makes design feel achievable and enjoyable — not intimidating or exclusive.
